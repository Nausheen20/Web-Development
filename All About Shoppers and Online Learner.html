Types of Shoppers
Image Shoppers Description
The Impulse Buyer Impulse buyer are master of buying thing. They always have a good reason to buy most of the things they see. They are likely to overspend and use credit cards-often over and over again. They usually don't careabout the price tag and will pay more for something than they should.
The Bargain Hunter These type of buyers are always armed with coupons, deals, online codes and reward card credits. They are expert in getting the best deals and can often form a reputation among friends and family.
The Brand Loyalist A good example of this could be seen in the Apple store where brand loyalist would wait outside the Apple store for nights before the release of a new iPhone. They do not really need any necessary product but since they are a loyal buyer they will definite buy something that is of that brand becuase they have pledged to buy products of that brand.
The Educated Shopper From among all the types of shoppers, this type of shopper is the toughest shopper to sell an itmen to. An educated shopper is a chronic researcher and does extensive investigation into whatever item they are looking for purchasing. Those type of shopper knows exactly what they want and is also very likely to have tracked down the best possible price on the desired product.
